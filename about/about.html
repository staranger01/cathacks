Initiatives
Since the Coronavirus first emerged,
we knew that we needed to do
something to help.
This website is built for your community. In it, people will be able to shop locally
from each other through a contactless experience. By doing this, people will
be able to obtain COVID Coins to spend on prizes and donations for a more
motivational experience. This website, will reduce the amount of people in grocery
stores as people will rely more on the community and other means of getting
their groceries. Furthermore, all profits made will be used to
fund hospitals and to help the people on the front line risking there lives for others.
Furthermore, customers can also directly donate from the app to their preferred
charity. Lets do our part and flatten the curve!
